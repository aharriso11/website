How great it is to be back in Hong Kong and temporarily away from the endless onslaught of rain that is winter in the UK. I’ve started this trip with a wander around the hilly Mid-Levels district using the impressive system of public escalators that ascends from Des Voeux Road, nominally following the path of Cochrane Street and Shelley Street, both of which have steps along much of their length.
