I have an automatic weather station located at 115m asl just to the north of the town of Weymouth on the UK south coast, monitoring local temperature, humidity and pressure.

## The weather station

The weather station is an Ecowitt GW1100 + WH32 temperature and humidity sensor, connected to a Raspberry Pi running the [Weewx weather station server](https://weewx.com/). It is situated at 115 metres above sea level in a south-facing garden, just over two miles away from and within clear sight of the English Channel.

You can [view the latest data from the weather station](http://dt3weather.s3-website.eu-central-1.amazonaws.com/index.html) (updated on an hourly basis).

## Useful links

### Observations

- [Weather Obs](http://weatherobs.com/) - mapped global weather observations, sourced from METAR and SYNOP observations, ship and buoy observations, and the citizen weather observer programme.
- [Sea water temperatures](https://www.wetter3.de/Fax/00_DWD_Wassertemps.gif) - hosted by German weather website wetter3.de
- [Gaisma](https://www.gaisma.com/en/location/poole.html) - sunrise, sunset, dawn and dusk times around the world

### Charts

- [Wetterzentrale - Top Karten](https://www.wetterzentrale.de/) - "Top Charts" is one of the biggest and best sites for free charts, if a little clunky. It's all in German but is reasonably easy to make sense of.
- [Wetter3](https://www.wetter3.de/) - another very good German weather website
- [The Weather Outlook](https://www.theweatheroutlook.com/) - an excellent UK weather website
- [Meteociel.fr](https://www.meteociel.fr/) - a useful French weather website including model charts and observations
- [WXMAP](https://www.fnmoc.navy.mil/wxmap_cgi/index.html) - another good chart site, hosted by the US Navy Oceanography portal.
- [Jet Stream forecasts](https://www.theweatheroutlook.com/twodata/datukgfshires.aspx?display=jetstream&model=gfs) - hosted by The Weather Outlook.
- [Passage Weather](https://www.passageweather.com/) - sailing forecasts, with useful SWH (significant wave height) charts
