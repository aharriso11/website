Here is a selection of blogs I read regularly, usually because I find them personally or professionally interesting. I may not agree with all the authors, or all the content but it's important that we read and experience opinions that are different from our own, isn't it?

I collect all these blogs into a news aggregator website called [Feedly](https://feedly.com/) which presents them all to me in one place, through a common interface. This means I can easily browse for something interesting to read, and save the ones I want to come back to later safe in the knowledge that I won't lose it. Prior to Feedly I used Bloglines, and Google Reader before that. Both these services got shut down which says something about the fickle nature of free services on the internet and my fondness for technical boat anchors.
